♡ about korilakkuma! ♡

♥ Korilakkuma is a white bear cub character created by San-X, introduced in 2003 as part of the Rilakkuma series. Korilakkuma's name combines "ko" (little), "rilak" (relax), and "kuma" (bear), and she is often seen with a red button on her chest, suggesting she may not be a real bear.

♥ In the Netflix series Rilakkuma and Kaoru, Korilakkuma is introduced as a mischievous, white bear cub who suddenly appears at Kaoru's apartment, just like Rilakkuma.
