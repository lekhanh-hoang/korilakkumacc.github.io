♡ tips for caring for your plushies! ♡

♥ since most collectors like to keep plush's tag to show authenticity, it is not recommended to wash the bears.

♥ bears are usually kept in a clear plastic bag to prevent dust. i find that the plastic bags that are used to protect documents are the most accessible.

♥ here are the plastic bag sizes that i use to store my different bears:

bear size bear height bag size
mascot 4 to 5 inches A5
small 6 to 7 inches A4
medium 11 to 12 inches B4

♥ if you need to wash your bear, hand washing with cold water and drying with cold settings is recommended! never machine wash bears!
